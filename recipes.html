Ricepudding Ingredients

 Rice
Milk
Water
Sugar
Salt
Vanilla extract(optional)
Cinnamon or nutmeg(optional)
Raisins(optional)

Preparation & cooking

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and drain it.
  2. Measure and keep all ingredients ready.
  3. Add rice and water to a saucepan and cook until the water is absorbed.
  4. Pour in the milk and add the salt.
  5. Simmer on low heat, stirring frequently.
  6. Add the sugar and cook until the pudding thickens.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  8. Add raisins or cinnamon if desired.
  9. Add raisins or cinnamon if desired.
